Main features


Today’s businesses are facing increasing challenges: fierce competition and budget cuts. With the SP C220S/SP C221SF/SP C222SF, Nashuatec presents three value-packed office devices that deliver affordable multitasking. Compact and easy to use, they provide the tools for fast-paced office productivity in accurate, attractive colour.

SP C220S
  • Fast B/W & colour output: 16 ppm.
  • Standard GDI/NIC.
SP C221SF
  • Fast B/W & colour output: 20 ppm.
  • Standard GDI/NIC.
  • Standard auto duplex.
  • Business faxing.
  • Automatic Document Feeder.
SP C222SF
  • Fast B/W & colour output: 20 ppm.
  • Standard PCL/PostScript® 3™/NIC.
  • Standard auto duplex.
  • Business faxing.
  • Automatic Document Feeder.
